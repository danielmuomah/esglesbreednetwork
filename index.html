About Daniel C Muomah

Daniel C Muomah is a minister with a passion for the Word of God, prayer, and Spirit-led discipleship. His ministry expresses the Gospel with clarity and power—calling men and women to salvation, consecration, and victorious living through Jesus Christ.

  • Personality: prophetic, prayerful, disciplined, compassionate, and Word-driven
  • Emphasis: holiness, spiritual growth, healing, deliverance, and kingdom living
  • Burden: raising believers who carry a burning altar of prayer and intimacy with God
