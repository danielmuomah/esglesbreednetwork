03 — Walking in Divine Alignment

Text: Hebrews 12:1–2

Key Truths

  • Alignment begins with focus: “looking unto Jesus.”
  • Weights are not always sins — distractions can derail destiny.
  • Endurance is a spiritual skill; you train it by discipline.

Practical Steps

  • Cut one distraction this week (time-waster, wrong company, unhealthy habit).
  • Choose one destiny habit: Bible reading, prayer, learning, skill growth.
  • Seek counsel and spiritual covering when decisions are heavy.

Prayer Points

  • Father, align my heart, my steps, and my decisions with Your will.
  • Every distraction assigned to slow me down, be broken in Jesus’ Name.
  • I receive grace to finish my race with joy and accuracy.
