11 — Family & Parenting

Goal: To build a godly home and raise children in wisdom, discipline, and purpose.

Key Scriptures

  • Proverbs 22:6 — Train up a child in the way he should go.
  • Deuteronomy 6:6–7 — Teach God’s Word diligently at home.
  • Ephesians 6:4 — Bring children up in nurture and admonition of the Lord.
  • Psalm 127:3 — Children are an heritage of the Lord.

Truths to Note

  • Children learn by instruction and by example.
  • Discipline is love with structure — not anger.
  • Prayer over children is spiritual covering and destiny preservation.

Practical Steps

  • Set a weekly family devotion time (15–30 mins).
  • Build routines: respect, chores, study, prayer, gratitude.
  • Speak blessings over your children daily.

Prayer Points

  • Lord, grant my home peace, love, and unity.
  • My children shall be taught of the Lord and great shall be their peace.
  • Every evil influence around my children, be destroyed in Jesus’ Name.
