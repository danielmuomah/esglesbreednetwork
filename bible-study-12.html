12 — Finishing Strong & Divine Preservation

Goal: To finish seasons well, avoid last-minute disaster, and walk in divine covering.

Key Scriptures

  • 2 Timothy 4:7 — “I have fought a good fight… I have finished my course…”
  • Psalm 121:7–8 — The Lord preserves your going out and coming in.
  • Ecclesiastes 7:8 — Better is the end of a thing than the beginning.
  • Psalm 91:1–2 — Dwelling in His secret place brings covering.

Truths to Note

  • Many start well; finishing well requires discipline and grace.
  • Preservation is both spiritual and practical (wisdom, caution, prayer).
  • Consistency is the secret of finishing strong.

Prayer Points

  • Father, establish me and help me finish every assignment well.
  • I reject sudden disaster, premature loss, and evil reports.
  • Let divine preservation surround me and my household, in Jesus’ Name.
