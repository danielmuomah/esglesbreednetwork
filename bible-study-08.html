08 — Healing, Deliverance & Freedom

Goal: To know God’s provision for healing and to stand in deliverance and liberty.

Key Scriptures

  • Isaiah 53:5 — “With His stripes we are healed.”
  • Psalm 107:20 — He sent His Word and healed them.
  • Luke 10:19 — Authority over all the power of the enemy.
  • John 8:36 — If the Son sets you free, you are free indeed.

Truths to Note

  • Healing is in the atonement; receive by faith and prayer.
  • Deliverance involves truth, repentance, prayer, and maintaining purity.
  • Freedom must be guarded: avoid doors that reopen bondage.

Practical Steps

  • Renounce known sins and close spiritual doors.
  • Pray scriptures daily and maintain fellowship.
  • Replace fear with the Word; speak healing and liberty.

Prayer Points

  • Every affliction in my body, dry up by the power of God.
  • Every yoke of bondage, break now in Jesus’ Name.
  • I declare liberty in my spirit, soul, and body.
