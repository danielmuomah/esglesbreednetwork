05 — Discipleship & Spiritual Growth

Goal: To grow from believer to disciple, stable and fruitful.

Key Scriptures

  • Matthew 28:19–20 — Make disciples of all nations.
  • Acts 2:42 — They continued in doctrine, fellowship, prayers.
  • 2 Timothy 2:2 — Teach faithful people who will teach others.
  • John 15:5 — Abiding in Christ produces fruit.

Discipleship Habits

  • Daily Word intake (read, study, meditate).
  • Prayer discipline and obedience to promptings.
  • Accountability and submission to spiritual guidance.
  • Service: ministry, giving, evangelism.

Prayer Points

  • Father, establish me in doctrine and spiritual maturity.
  • Let my life bring many into Christ and into growth.
  • I receive grace to finish well and bear fruit that remains.
