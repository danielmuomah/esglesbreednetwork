04 — Marriage: Before You Marry

Goal: To prepare wisely for marriage and avoid destiny errors.

Key Scriptures

  • Amos 3:3 — Can two walk together except they be agreed?
  • Proverbs 4:23 — Guard your heart with all diligence.
  • 2 Corinthians 6:14 — Do not be unequally yoked.
  • Genesis 2:18 — Purpose of companionship and help.

Before You Marry — Check These

  • Spiritual Compatibility: salvation, values, prayer life.
  • Purpose Alignment: destiny, calling, direction.
  • Character Proof: humility, honesty, anger control, integrity.
  • Family Wisdom: seek counsel, avoid secrecy.

Prayer Points

  • Father, deliver me from wrong choices and wrong connections.
  • Let my heart be guided by Your Spirit, not pressure.
  • I receive wisdom to build a godly home, in Jesus’ Name.
