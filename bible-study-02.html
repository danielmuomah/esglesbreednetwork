02 — Prayer & Spiritual Authority

Goal: To build a consistent prayer altar and understand authority in Christ.

Key Scriptures

  • Luke 18:1 — Men ought always to pray and not faint.
  • James 5:16 — The effectual fervent prayer of the righteous avails much.
  • Luke 10:19 — Authority over all the power of the enemy.
  • Ephesians 6:10–12 — The reality of spiritual warfare.

Prayer Altar Basics

  • Set a fixed time daily (morning or night) and guard it.
  • Combine Word + Worship + Prayer + Listening.
  • Pray with scriptures; don’t pray empty words.

Prayer Points

  • Father, baptize my heart with fresh fire and consistency.
  • Every power resisting my destiny, scatter by fire in Jesus’ Name.
  • I receive grace to pray through until answers manifest.
