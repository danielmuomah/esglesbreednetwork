01 — Salvation & New Birth

Goal: To understand repentance, faith in Christ, and assurance of salvation.

Key Scriptures

  • John 3:3 — “Except a man be born again, he cannot see the kingdom of God.”
  • Romans 10:9–10 — Confess Jesus as Lord and believe God raised Him.
  • Ephesians 2:8–9 — Saved by grace through faith, not by works.
  • 1 John 5:13 — You can know you have eternal life.

Truths to Note

  • Salvation is God’s gift — not human achievement.
  • Repentance is a change of mind that produces a change of life.
  • New birth gives a new nature, new desires, and new direction.

Practical Application

  • Write your salvation testimony in 5–10 lines.
  • Identify one old habit to drop and one new habit to build this week.
  • Begin daily Bible reading (start with John + Romans).

Prayer Points

  • Lord Jesus, strengthen my faith and keep me grounded in Your grace.
  • Let the power of the new birth produce holiness and consistency in me.
  • I receive grace to grow and bear fruit that remains, in Jesus’ Name.
