10 — Kingdom Prosperity & Stewardship

Goal: To understand prosperity as covenant stewardship — resources managed for Kingdom impact.

Key Scriptures

  • Deuteronomy 8:18 — Power to get wealth to establish covenant.
  • Genesis 26:12–14 — Isaac sowed and prospered by God’s blessing.
  • Proverbs 10:22 — The blessing of the Lord makes rich.
  • Luke 16:10–12 — Faithfulness in little qualifies for much.

Truths to Note

  • Prosperity must produce generosity and responsibility.
  • Stewardship involves budgeting, saving, giving, and diligence.
  • God prospers you to be a blessing, not a burden.

Practical Steps

  • Track your income/expenses for 7 days.
  • Create a simple plan: giving + savings + needs + growth.
  • Ask God for wisdom for profit, integrity, and increase.

Prayer Points

  • Father, bless the work of my hands and establish me in increase.
  • Deliver me from waste and grant me stewardship wisdom.
  • Let my resources advance the Kingdom, in Jesus’ Name.
