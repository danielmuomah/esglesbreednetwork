06 — The Holy Spirit & His Gifts

Goal: To know the Person of the Holy Spirit and walk in His power with balance and obedience.

Key Scriptures

  • Acts 1:8 — “Ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you…”
  • John 14:26 — He teaches and reminds us of truth.
  • Galatians 5:22–23 — Fruit of the Spirit (character).
  • 1 Corinthians 12:4–11 — Gifts of the Spirit (operations).

Truths to Note

  • The Spirit is a Person: He leads, comforts, corrects, empowers.
  • Gifts are powerful, but fruit is proof of maturity.
  • Power must be governed by Scripture, love, and holiness.

Practice This

  • Daily: “Holy Spirit, lead me today.” Then obey promptings.
  • Study 1 Corinthians 12–14 for balance and order.
  • Ask God to strengthen both character (fruit) and impact (gifts).

Prayer Points

  • Holy Spirit, fill me afresh and make me sensitive to Your voice.
  • Let Your gifts operate in me for the edification of the Church.
  • Let the fruit of the Spirit be evident in my life, in Jesus’ Name.
